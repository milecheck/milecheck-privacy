Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

MileCheck ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Your precise location data stays on your device. We do not sell your data or share personally identifiable information with third parties.

Information We Access

Location Data

The App accesses your device's GPS location to:

  • Display your nearest mile marker - The core function of the app
  • Show your current speed and direction - Helps you navigate
  • Auto-detect your state - Loads the correct milepost data for your region
  • Log trip sessions - If you choose to use the trip logging feature

Important: Your precise GPS coordinates are processed on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Trip Log Data

If you use the trip logging feature, the App stores:

  • Timestamps of logged locations
  • Mile marker positions during your trip
  • Route information

All trip data is stored locally on your device only.

How Your Data is Stored

  • Location data - Processed on your device in real-time, not stored permanently or transmitted
  • Trip logs - Stored locally on your device. You can clear all trip history from the Log tab at any time.
  • Settings preferences - Stored locally (selected state, theme, time format, etc.)

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • National Weather Service API - Weather data for your area (your approximate location is sent to retrieve weather)
  • Google Maps - Map tiles for the interactive map on Android
  • Apple Maps - Map tiles for the interactive map on iOS
  • State DOT APIs - Road condition and incident data (Oregon TripCheck, Washington WSDOT, Montana 511, Arizona DOT). No personal data is sent to these services; requests are proxied through our server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete - Clear all local data by clearing trip history in the app or uninstalling
  • Deny location - You can deny location access at any time through your device settings

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is intended for drivers and travelers of legal driving age.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Use "Clear All Trip History" in the Log tab to remove trip data
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • No data is stored on our servers that needs to be deleted

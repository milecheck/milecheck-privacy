Privacy Policy

Last updated April 27, 2026

MileCheck (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary

Your precise location data stays on your device. We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports to improve stability. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple and Google, not by MileCheck. We do not sell your data or share personally identifiable information with third parties.

Information We Access

Location Data

The App accesses your device’s GPS location to:

  • Display your nearest mile marker — the core function of the app
  • Show your current speed and direction — helps you navigate
  • Auto-detect your state — loads the correct milepost data for your region
  • Log trip sessions — if you choose to use the trip logging feature

Important: Your precise GPS coordinates are processed on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Trip Log Data

If you use the trip logging feature, the App stores:

  • Timestamps of logged locations
  • Mile marker positions during your trip
  • Route information
  • Any notes you add to a trip

Trip data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual trips or clear all trip history from the Log tab at any time.

Subscriptions and Purchases

MileCheck offers an optional premium subscription to unlock additional features (map, alerts, and trip logging). Here is how subscription data is handled:

  • Payment processing — All purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). MileCheck never receives, stores, or has access to your payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.
  • Subscription management — We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status across platforms. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and your subscription status (active, expired, trial) from Apple or Google. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment details.
  • What RevenueCat stores — Anonymous user identifier, subscription status, purchase dates, and platform (iOS/Android). See RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.
  • Free trial — Trial eligibility is determined by Apple or Google based on your Apple ID or Google account, not by MileCheck.
  • Managing your subscription — You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device’s App Store or Google Play settings.

Crash Reports and Error Logging

We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and error logs to fix bugs and improve stability.

What Sentry receives
  • App version and device model/OS version
  • Error stack traces
  • Anonymous breadcrumbs of in-app actions (e.g., “opened Map tab,” “tapped Premium button”) that help us reproduce bugs

What Sentry does NOT receive:

  • Your GPS location or coordinates
  • Your trip log data or travel history
  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Payment or subscription details
  • Any personally identifiable information

We do not currently collect product or usage analytics (screen views, session counts, feature usage). We may add analytics in a future update and will update this policy if we do.

How Your Data is Stored

  • Location data — Processed on your device in real-time, not stored permanently or transmitted
  • Trip logs — Stored locally on your device. You control when to delete them.
  • Settings preferences — Stored locally (theme, display options, etc.)
  • Subscription status — Cached locally by RevenueCat SDK for offline access. Synced with Apple/Google servers when online.
  • Crash reports — Sent to Sentry for error analysis, anonymized, stored for 90 days.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Sentry — Anonymous crash reports and error logs to improve app stability. See Sentry’s Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — Map display on both iOS and Android. See Google’s Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management service. Processes anonymous subscription data. See RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — Process subscription purchases. See Apple’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Privacy Policy
  • State DOT APIs — Road condition data (Washington WSDOT, Oregon TripCheck, Montana 511, Arizona DOT, Utah UDOT, Idaho 511, Alaska 511, Colorado COtrip). No personal data is sent to these services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request information about data we have collected
  • Delete — Clear all local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt-out of crash reporting — Contact info@milecheckapp.com to request deletion of any stored crash data
  • Cancel subscription — Manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store or Google Play settings

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is intended for drivers and travelers of legal driving age.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Trip logs can be deleted individually or all at once from the Log tab
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data (trips, settings, cached subscription status)
  • Crash reports sent to Sentry are anonymized and automatically deleted after 90 days
  • To cancel your subscription, use your device’s App Store or Google Play subscription settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
